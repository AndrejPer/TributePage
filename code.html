Dr. Nikola Tesla

The man who tamed the lightning

Nikola Tesla holding a wirelessly charched light bulb

Here is a time line of Dr Nikola Tesla's life:

  • 1856 - born in the Austrian Empire, modern-day Croatia
  • 1873 - graduated from High School in Karlovci, Austrian Empire, one year ahead of his class
  • 1875 - enrolled at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz, Austria, considered the most brilliant student of the generation, later abandoned studies
  • 1881 - moved to Budapest, Hungary, to work at a telegraph company
  • 1882 - moved to Paris to work for Edison Continental Company
  • 1884 - emigration to United States
  • 1885 - created the Tesla Electric Light & Manufacturing
  • 1888 - started working at Westinghouse Electric
  • 1891 - become a naturalised citizen of United States; patented his Tesla coil
  • 1892 - together with Westinghouse Electric showcased the "Tesla Polyphase System" at the World's Columbian Exposition in Chicago
  • 1943 - died on Orthodox Christmass Eve in New York
